🎮 Level & Prestige System
-levelinfo ▼
Shows how many levels exist and when prestige becomes available.
-prestigeinfo ▼
Lists all prestige symbols and explains the XP bonus per prestige.
-prestige ▼
Resets you to Level 1 after Level 15, increases your prestige level and permanently gives you more XP per action.
💰 Moodi & Gaming
-moodi / -m ▼
Grind Moodi, earn XP and Moodi (with streak and prestige bonus, 1h cooldown).
-case ▼
Opens a virtual CS:GO case for 200 Moodi. 60s cooldown per user.
-steal / -s ▼
Try to steal Moodi from a random user.
-gamble <Amount|all|half> ▼
Bet Moodi and either double or lose it with luck.
-daily ▼
Daily bonus (Moodi & XP, once per day).
📊 Stats & Leaderboards
-points ▼
Shows your (or a user's) Moodi, XP, Level, Prestige and Streak.
-stats ▼
Shows detailed stats (Moodi, XP, Level, Streak, last Moodi).
-top ▼
Shows the top 5 users by Moodi.
-toplevel ▼
Shows the top 5 users by Level/XP.
😴 Status Commands
-afk [Reason] ▼
Sets you as AFK, optionally with a reason.
-gn [Reason] ▼
Sets you as sleeping, optionally with a reason.
-remind <User|me> <Time> <Message> ▼
Reminds you or another user after the specified time automatically in chat.
Without time, the message is displayed when the user next writes.
